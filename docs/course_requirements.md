# Course and credit requirements

## Graduate-level standing

All courses counted toward the MEng must be at the 5000 or 6000 level. Courses
at the 4000 level taken during the MEng do not count toward the credit total,
even if the course is offered cross-listed at both levels.

## Course load per term

Full-time students typically register for 1.5 to 2.0 credits per term, which
corresponds to three or four half-credit courses. The minimum to maintain
full-time status is 1.0 credits per term. Part-time students may register for
0.5 credits per term.

## Grade requirements

A minimum grade of C+ is required in each course counted toward the MEng. A
cumulative GPA of 7.0 (B-) or higher must be maintained throughout the program.
Students whose GPA falls below 7.0 are placed on academic probation. Two
consecutive terms on probation results in withdrawal from the program.

## Repeating courses

A failed course may be repeated once. A passed course with a grade below B
may be repeated once with permission of the graduate supervisor. Only the
higher of the two grades is used in the GPA calculation.

## Transfer credits

Up to 1.0 credits may be transferred from another accredited graduate program,
subject to approval by the MEng coordinator and the Faculty of Graduate
Studies. Transferred credits must have been earned within the five years
preceding admission to Carleton.
